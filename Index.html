A Weekend at Rio

Rio de Janeiro metropolitan area and the second-most populous municipality in Brazil and the sixth-most populous in the Americas. Rio de Janeiro is the capital of the state of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil's third-most populous state. Part of the city has been designated as a World Heritage Site.

I stayed three days visiting Rio this month! I was able to see many things while we were there!

Day 1

On my first day, I started by visiting Rio Downtown right away. My second stop was the statue of Christ the Redeemer (check out that beautiful picture!).

Day 2

On my second day, I went to visit statue of Christ the Redeemer on Corcovado;. An extremely popular hot spot for tourist, to visit and take pictures.

After I admired the view, I went back done from the top of Corcovado, and visited another beautiful mountain overlooking the city of Rio, Such as Sugarloaf Mountain. Sugarloaf Mountain, has arguably one of the best views of the city possible, as seen with the pictures. subsequently, I visited Parque Enrique Lage.

Day 3

The picture below is of me visiting the Parque Lage. It's huge! I also went to Visual Arts School of Parque Lage. Seeing the work of series of brazilian television shows, the staging of well-known plays such as Shakespeare's "The Tempest", in person was extremely interesting. I was surprised to see that the museum also had many people interested in establishing or deepening their contact with art.
